The Best Bars and Restaurants in Newcastle

Newcastle has got quite a lot going for it these days. From the famous Bridges, the Magpies on the come up, Newkie Brown Ale, the infamous Geordie Shore and of course the 'Geordie' dialect itself. What a lot of people know it for, which sadly Geordie Shore promoted all too well, is its vibrant and lively nightlife. With over 265 drinking estableshments in its compact city its no wonder that its bars are always full on the weekend, come rain or snow.
